How it works?

Do you remember the old tamagotchi games? Where do you need to take care of your pet: feed it, caress it, play with it?

We thought, why not make a similar concept on blockchain with 2d tyan so that you can talk, feed, caress and play with your girl and earn money for it?

Of course, that's not all! Each girl can be dressed the way you want (and the way she wants, of course!), choose the best room for her, and she will delight you every day with her presence ^-^

Interface & Gameplay alpha
Balance 1028 LUV Caressing Chating Eating Playing market change items
1

First of all, gameplay is interaction through 4 main actions: Caressing, Chating, Eating & Playing. Every hour you can choose action, what you want to perform. LUV tokens will be credited to you for each action.

Choose wisely, cause depending on what kind of character your girl has, what her mood now and how much time is on the clock (yes, this is also important), your earnings will be different.

Caressing Chating Eating Playing market
2

If you do everything right, you will see an increase in your girl's mood, which will have a positive effect on your earnings. Just look, what the reaction to your actions is. And, of course, you will get acquainted with the lore of each girl, so that it will be easier for you to understand her ;3

If the mood scale goes down, you should reconsider your game strategy. About which variables affect mood - read more in Litepaper

3

Your equip (hairstyle, clothes and room) play a key role in determining the size of your LUV earnings. Each girl has default attributes that are not nft, but if you want to get more tokens and be able to customize your waifu, you need to change standart attributes to something better.

Read more about calculating the amount of your earnings and equip in our Litepaper

Roadmap
This is the alpha version, please note that the roadmap may change or modify over time, follow our announcements!
November 2021
Make the idea&concept
We came up with and developed the concept of the game. We were inspired by various anime masterpieces, the game "Endless Summer", images from real life. Mechanics, economic system, interactions and plot – are all here.
February 2022
Launch social media
A website, twitter, discord has been created. The first activity is started. Contests, sweepstakes.
March/April 2022
Start of sales nft packs
By March, we expect to start the first sales of our packs, they will take place in several waves: first free promos, then the main sales of girls and items.
April/May 2022
Lauch Alpha version
We are planning to release an alpha version of the game, which, nevertheless, everyone who has a girl's NFT will have the opportunity to play and test any bugs and errors.
May 2022
Beta version
By the end of the month, the game should be released in beta, where all the necessary mechanics will work and you can start earning by playing.
June/July 2022
Add a mini-games
Mini-game mechanics will be added, in which you can play against live users and bet earned LUV, winning in duels. Adding a weekly leaderboard.
